About us

The VayuMet Weather web application has been developed by Air Cmde Neeraj Sharma, an Air Force veteran with over 35 years of experience in operational military aviation. He used his knowledge and experience gained to create high-quality web application that displays meteorological data from around the world and allows you to monitor weather development for any place on earth. At VayuMet, the focus is on weather prediction and meteorological data visualisation on the aspects and details which are crucial to various area ranging from aviation, agriculture, risk & disaster management and sustainable energy to services sectors like tourism and market place at one place. The VayuMet web application is accessible to anyone around the world, aimed at improving awareness about meteorological events in our atmosphere. Images from the application can be freely redistributed with source and thus help us achieve our goal.
